# Privacy Policy — Focus Guard

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

## Data Collection

Focus Guard does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## What data is stored locally

- **Extension enabled/disabled state** — stored in Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`)
- **Per-site toggle preferences** (YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram) — stored locally
- **Daily block count** — a simple counter reset each day, stored locally

## Data sharing

Focus Guard does **not**:
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking
- Access browsing history beyond the specific sites it blocks (YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram)
- Collect personal information

## Permissions explained

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Save your on/off preferences locally |
| `declarativeNetRequest` | Block navigation to Shorts/Reels/TikTok URLs |
| Host permissions (youtube.com, facebook.com, tiktok.com, instagram.com) | Inject content script to hide Reels/Shorts elements in the page |
